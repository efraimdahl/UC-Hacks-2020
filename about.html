About Us

Change is hard, be ready

As our population becomes increasingly more urban, old challenges will be amplified and new ones will arrise. Not just in cities where re-urbanization is necessary but difficult, but also in rural areas where population decline will make it harder and more expensive to maintain infrastructure and services in disparate regions.

Our team is focused on helping communities of all sizes prepare and adapt to these changes through shared mobility solutions and the infrastructure required for an EV revolution.
